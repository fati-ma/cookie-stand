Stores Opening Hours



You can shop 7 days a week in Rotterdam. Only the hours differ per day, on Monday the shops open later and on Friday there is late-night shopping.

Measures coronavirus

In response to the coronavirus the government has taken additional measures. Some stores have adjusted their opening hours and others are closed until April 6 at least. Therefore, keep a close eye on the social media and websites of different stores for current information.

Read more info about the measures to contain the coronavirus.

City Centres

Most of the shops in the city centre open at 10:00 until 18:00, and at Friday until 21:00. The Markthal is open until 20:00 nearly every day and until 21:00 on Friday and 18:00 on Sunday. Department stores, like Dubai and Lima, are almost daily open until 19.00.
